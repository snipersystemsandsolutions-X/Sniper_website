Our Buyback Process

1

Evaluation

Submit details about your IT assets through our online form or contact us directly. Provide information about device types, models, quantities, and condition.

2

Quotation

We evaluate assets and provide a competitive quote based on market value, condition, and specifications. No obligation to proceed.

3

Pick Up

Once you accept the quote, we arrange pickup with proper documentation and chain-of-custody protocols.

4

Secure Data Wiping

All devices undergo certified data sanitization using industry-standard methods and we provide certificates for compliance.

5

Payment

After verification, payment is released according to agreed terms. We offer flexible options including bank transfer.

6

Certificate Distribution

Devices that meet resale or reuse criteria are refurbished and redistributed through certified channels, ensuring compliance, quality, and environmental responsibility.
