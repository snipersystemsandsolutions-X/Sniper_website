Multipurpose Innovation Lab Powered by Mac & AATCE
Transform Learning. Empower Innovation. Build Future-Ready Talent.

The Multipurpose Innovation Lab is a next-generation learning space for universities and colleges that integrates industry-grade tools, skill-based learning, and global Apple certifications. Powered by Mac and aligned with the Apple Authorised Training Centre for Education (AATCE) framework, the lab brings creativity, technology, and career-oriented learning under one ecosystem.

Talk to an Expert

Why a Multipurpose Innovation Lab?

Modern industries demand graduates who can think creatively, design intelligently, and innovate rapidly. With Mac at the center of the lab, institutions can support a wide array of disciplines — from engineering to design, media, and research — using world-class software and Apple's professional learning programs. This lab becomes a single hub for learning, creation, collaboration, and certification.

Key Capabilities of the Lab

Industry-Leading Performance with Mac

Mac provides unmatched power, reliability, and security for higher education workflows. Students can run:

  • Autodesk Fusion 360
  • Adobe Creative Cloud (Photoshop, Illustrator, Premiere Pro, XD)
  • MATLAB & Simulink
  • Xcode for App Development
  • Final Cut Pro & Logic Pro
  • Blender, Unity, Unreal Engine
  • Python, R, and developer tools

Supports engineering, design, architecture, media studies, computer science, and management.

Apple Authorised Training Centre for Education (AATCE)

Enable official Apple curriculum and certification to make students globally competitive.

  • App Development with Swift
  • Final Cut Pro
  • Logic Pro
  • Apple Teacher & Education Resources
  • On-campus Apple certification exams
  • Faculty enablement and professional development
  • Curriculum integration for credit-based and skill-based programs
  • Recognised branding as an AATCE
