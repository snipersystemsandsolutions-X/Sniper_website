Security and Privacy in Education
A Safe Digital Environment for Every Learner

At Sniper education, in partnership with Apple, we believe that technology in education should empower students — not compromise their safety. Every iPad and Mac is built with industry-leading privacy and security protections designed to keep personal information secure and learning environments safe.

Talk to an Expert Explore Training Session

Built-in Privacy, Right from the Start

Apple devices are designed with privacy at the core, not as an add-on. From the first time a student signs in, their personal data is encrypted and stored securely. No personal information is ever shared with third parties for advertising or tracking, and parents can be confident that their child’s data remains private and protected.

Secure Authentication

Managed Apple IDs give each student a unique login controlled by the institution, enabling secure access and clear ownership.

Data Encryption

All stored and transmitted data is encrypted automatically, helping protect sensitive information at rest and in transit.

No Tracking

Apple does not build profiles based on student activity or sell user data — ensuring learning activity is private and purpose-driven.

App Transparency

Apps request permissions before accessing sensitive information; schools can review and control which apps are permitted in classrooms.

Parental Peace of Mind

Sniper education’s Apple ecosystem helps schools create safe, age-appropriate digital spaces that parents can trust. Through features such as Screen Time, Family Sharing, and App Restrictions, families can monitor usage, set time limits, and approve apps before installation — maintaining a healthy balance between learning and digital wellbeing.

  • View and manage device activity — Parents and guardians can review device usage and activity in line with school policies.
  • Approve downloads and purchases — Families can approve apps and in-app purchases remotely.
  • Monitor online behaviour — Tools to observe and guide safe online habits while respecting privacy.
  • Support responsible device use — Policies and tools that work both at home and in school.

Safe Collaboration and Classroom Management

Teachers can guide lessons, share materials, and manage devices through Apple Classroom and Schoolwork — all within a secure environment that keeps student information private. Sniper education ensures every deployment complies with institutional data policies and national standards for digital learning security.

Institutional Control

Devices are configured via Apple School Manager and MDM to ensure institutional control and consistent security policies.

Authorized Access

Only Premium users and verified accounts access educational apps and content, reducing risk of unauthorized data exposure.

Remote Management

Lost or misplaced devices can be locked or erased remotely, protecting all stored data and preserving student privacy.

Policy Compliance

Deployments are aligned with institutional data policies and applicable national or regional standards.

Our Commitment to Digital Safety

Sniper education’s education solutions focus on three key principles to keep learners safe and data protected:

  • Student-First Privacy – No data collection beyond what’s essential for learning.
  • Secure Learning Environments – Centralized device management and data encryption for every user.
  • Transparency for Families – Clear visibility for parents into how devices and apps are used for education.
