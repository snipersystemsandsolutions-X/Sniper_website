Apple Teacher Learning Center
Empower educators — transform learning

The Apple Teacher Learning Center is a free, self-paced professional learning program designed to help educators master Apple technology and transform teaching. From creating lessons with iPad and Mac to using Pages, Keynote, GarageBand, and iMovie — teachers gain hands-on experience that inspires student-centered learning.

Sniper Education, an Apple Business and Education Partner, helps schools adopt the Apple Teacher program — equipping educators to build confidence, creativity, and collaboration.

What Makes Apple Teacher Different

Professional growth for every educator — accessible learning and recognition that inspires.

Professional Growth for Every Educator

Whether new to technology or experienced, Apple Teacher helps educators confidently use iPad and Mac to create, collaborate, and teach with impact.

Learn Anytime, Anywhere

Accessible , the platform allows teachers to complete lessons and earn badges at their own pace — at school, home, or during professional learning sessions.

Hands-On Skill Development

Guided tutorials focus on creativity, productivity and classroom innovation — offering practical ideas for real-world teaching.


Globally Recognized Standards

Apple Teacher aligns with ISTE Standards for Educators, supporting innovation, digital citizenship, and impactful technology integration.

Collaborative Learning Culture

Join a global community of Apple Teachers using #AppleTeacher — share creative ideas, classroom projects, and innovative approaches with educators worldwide.

Recognition That Inspires

Each completed module earns digital badges and the Apple Teacher certification, celebrating educators’ commitment to excellence and lifelong learning.
