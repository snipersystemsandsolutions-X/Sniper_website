Apple Professional Learning Specialists

Empowering Educators. Inspiring Innovation. Enhancing Learning.

Transforming Education with Apple Technology

Apple Professional Learning Specialists (APLS) are experienced educators dedicated to helping schools and universities unlock the full potential of Apple technology for teaching and learning.

They work closely with educators, leaders, and IT professionals to design personalised professional learning plans that strengthen teaching practices, leadership strategies, and technology integration.

How APL Specialists Support Institutions

For Educators

Build skills, inspire instructional innovation, and enhance learning outcomes through continuous coaching.

For Leaders

Strengthen leadership capacity with strategic planning, executive coaching, and vision development.

For IT Teams

Align technology implementation with effective teaching and learning practices.

Our Approach

APL Specialists deliver learning experiences that are:

Personalised – Tailored to institutional goals and community needs.
Comprehensive – Covering teaching, leadership, and IT development.
Job-Embedded – Integrated into real classroom and institutional contexts.
Hands-On – Focusing on deep, practical application of Apple technology.
Sustained – Designed for ongoing support and long-term impact.

With Apple Professional Learning, institutions receive research-based guidance, collaborative support, and transformative learning experiences that inspire innovation and empower every learner.

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about APL sessions

How much does an APL session cost?

APL session pricing varies based on the type, duration, and format. We'll provide detailed pricing information when we contact you to confirm your session details.

What equipment do I need for a virtual session?

For virtual sessions, you'll need a reliable internet connection, a computer or tablet with camera and microphone, and any Apple devices you'd like to work with during the session.

Can you come to our school for on-site training?

Yes! We offer on-site training sessions at your location. This is particularly popular for group workshops and strategic planning sessions with leadership teams.

What if I need to reschedule my session?

We understand that schedules can change. Please contact us at least 24 hours in advance to reschedule your session without any fees.

Do you provide follow-up support after the session?

Absolutely! Every APL session includes 30 days of follow-up support via email, plus access to our resource library and implementation guides.
