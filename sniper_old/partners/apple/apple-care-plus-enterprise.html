Extended Repair and Replacement Coverage with AppleCare

Global Onsite Hardware Support

Gain peace of mind with flexible service plans lasting 2, 3, or 4 years from the date of your hardware purchase. Whether you need onsite support at your location or prefer to visit any Apple Authorized Service Provider within Apple's extensive global network, AppleCare ensures:

Rapid Response: Service as soon as the next business day for onsite issues.

Global Accessibility: Comprehensive service coverage worldwide.

Reliability: Minimized downtime and quick resolutions for hardware issues.

Extended Repair and Replacement Coverage

AppleCare for Enterprise provides additional assurance with extended coverage, including:

Repair or Replacement Benefits: Coverage for up to 4% of Mac computers or Apple-branded displays, and up to 10% of iPads or iPhones, at no extra cost.

Fast Turnaround Times: Repairs or replacements typically processed within a business day to minimize operational disruptions.

Comprehensive End-User Support

Relieve the burden on your IT help desk with end-user support, ensuring:

Assistance for Apple hardware, operating systems, and applications like Keynote, Pages, and Numbers.

Guidance for personal accounts and settings to streamline workflows.

Improved IT support experiences, allowing your teams to focus on core business tasks.

Dedicated IT Department Support

Strengthen your IT team with advanced support designed for enterprise needs, including:

Unlimited Telephone Assistance: Expert troubleshooting for hardware and software issues.

Annual IT-Level Support: Help with complex deployment and integration, such as MDM and Active Directory.

Flexible Support Plans: Tailored agreements to address occasional or regular IT needs.
