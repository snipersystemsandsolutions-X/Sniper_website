Jamf for Small Business

Small businesses need powerful tools — not complexity. With Jamf for Small Business , delivered by Sniper Systems & Solutions , you gain enterprise-grade Apple device management that's designed for smaller teams.

Simple Setup, Smarter Management

  • Get new Macs and iPhones up and running instantly with zero-touch deployment.

Your business data stays safe, always. Jamf's built-in protections keep devices compliant and secure with:
