Sniper Systems helps you set up and deploy your Apple devices throughout your organization with Apple Business Manager and third-party mobile device management (MDM) solution at any scale. Apple Business Manager automates enrollment of Apple devices while mobile device management (MDM) allows you to configure and manage devices and wirelessly share and manage applications.

Take Control With Apple Business Manager

Apple Business Manager is a web-based portal for IT administrators to deploy iPhone, iPad, iPod touch, Apple TV, and Mac all from one place. Apple Business Manager enables automated device enrolment, giving organizations a fast, streamlined way to deploy corporate-owned Apple devices and enroll in MDM without having to physically touch or prepare each device.

Manage and deploy BYOD or company-owned devices easily

With SNIPER and Apple, it’s easy to choose the right deployment option to meet the needs of your organization. Whether your employees are bringing their own devices to work with your BYOD program or you have company-owned devices, now you can protect and secure your company information at any level.

User enrolment for BYOD

If you have a BYOD program in your organization, your IT team can have control over the security of the devices limited to company and work data. Thus, not breaching the privacy of the employee.

MDM functions are limited on personal devices.

  • Configure accounts
  • Configure per App VPN
  • Install and configure apps
  • Require a passcode
  • Enforce certain restrictions
  • Access inventory of work apps
  • Remove work data only
  • Access personal information
  • Access inventory of personal apps
  • Remove any personal data
  • Collect any logs on the device
  • Take over personal apps
  • Require a complex passcode
  • Remotely wipe the entire device
  • Access device location

Managing and deploying company-owned devices

Your IT can take a higher level of control on company-owned devices and even automatically provision devices into MDM during setup, right out of the box. The IT team can manage and deploy additional security configurations, non-removable MDM and solutions, and manage the software updates.

IT has more control on company-owned Apple devices.

  • Configure accounts
  • Configure global proxies
  • Install, configure, and remove apps
  • Require a complex passcode
  • Enforce all restrictions
  • Access inventory of all apps
  • Remove erase the entire device
  • Manage software updates
  • Remove system apps
  • Modify the wallpaper
  • Lock into a single app
  • Bypass Activation lock
  • Force Wi-Fi on
  • Place device in Lost Mode
