Technology & Practices That Drive Sustainability

Apple's commitment to sustainability extends across every aspect of device design, manufacturing, and lifecycle management. These principles create a foundation for responsible school operations:

Energy-efficient devices: iPad and Mac are engineered for low power use, longer battery life, and efficient performance, reducing school energy demands.

Responsible materials & manufacturing: Apple designs with recycled metals, renewable energy and minimal environmental impact — supporting your school's green credentials.

Recycling & trade-in programmes: Schools can use trade-in value and recycling pathways to upgrade devices sustainably and responsibly.

Student-led sustainability initiatives: With Apple tools and resources, students can engage in projects around climate action, ecosystems and design thinking for sustainability.
