Frequently Asked Questions

Do Classroom and Schoolwork work together?

Yes — Classroom manages live sessions while Schoolwork handles assignments and progress tracking, both integrated through Apple School Manager.

Do teachers need separate accounts?

No — both apps work seamlessly with Managed Apple IDs configured through Apple School Manager.

Can teachers see what students are doing?

Yes, Apple Classroom allows teachers to view each student's screen and guide learning securely in real time.

Does it require Wi-Fi?

Yes, both teacher and student devices must be connected to the same Wi-Fi or managed network for Classroom to function properly.
