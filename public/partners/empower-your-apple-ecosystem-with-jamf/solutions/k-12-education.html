Jamf for K-12

Jamf for K–12 helps schools manage and secure Apple devices at scale while supporting classroom learning, promoting student equity and driving IT efficiency. Built for education, Jamf simplifies deployment, boosts teacher confidence and enhances the learning experience for every student.

Everything Schools Need — Simple, Secure, and Scalable

Simplified Device Deployment

  • Automate setup with Apple School Manager and zero-touch enrollment.
  • Configure, assign, and secure devices instantly — across grades, campuses, or districts.
  • Save IT time with pre-configured apps, Wi-Fi, and security settings.

Empower Teachers in the Classroom

  • Give teachers control with Jamf's classroom tools.
  • Restrict distractions (apps, camera, web) and keep lessons focused.
  • Instantly guide, lock, or share lessons through Apple Classroom integration.

Protect Students and Data

  • Jamf Protect continuously defends against malware and unsafe websites.
  • Web filtering & compliance policies safeguard students online.
  • Maintain visibility across every iPad and Mac with real-time reporting.

Unified Dashboard for IT

Manage all devices, users, and permissions from one console. Get compliance alerts, automate patch updates, and generate security reports effortlessly.
